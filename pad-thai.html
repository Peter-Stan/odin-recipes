Easy home made Pad Thai

Delicious homemade Pad Thai doesn't get any more doable than this. Create the perfect blend of Asian-inspired pure flavor with ginger and red pepper. Add crunchy peanut butter and toss with vegetables and noodles for a flavor-loaded dish that rivals Thai restaurant cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 200grams pad thai rice noodle
  • 2 teaspoons vegitable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons of crunchy peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of soysauce
  • 2 tablespoons of lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons of brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of ground ginger
  • I cup of bean sprouts

Steps

  1. Cook noodles as directed on package.
  2. Heat oil in wok or large skillet on medium-high heat. Add egg, cook and stir 30 seconds (egg will not be fully cooked).
  3. Stir in cooked noodles and remaining ingredients, except bean sprouts. Cook and stir until most of the liquid has been absorbed by the noodles, about 1 minute. Stir in bean sprouts. Serve with crushed peanuts, sliced green onions and lime wedges, if desired.
